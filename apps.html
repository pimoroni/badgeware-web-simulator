Running the apps

These are the apps that ship on the badge, plus community apps contributed by other makers. Click a tile (or its play button) to run that app right here in its own screen. Hit Edit to open its source in the editor and tinker. For the Python API and the full guides, head to badgewa.re/docs.

Pressing the badge's buttons

While an app is playing, your keyboard becomes the badge:

  • ←A
  • SpaceB
  • →C
  • ↑ ↓Up / Down
  • EscHome (returns to the launcher)

On a touchscreen

Swipe across the playing tile to work the D-pad, or tap it:

  • Swipe ← / →A / C
  • Swipe ↑ / ↓Up / Down
  • TapB
